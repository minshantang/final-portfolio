This year season is very special, since the Manchester United legend, Cristiano Ronaldo, has come home after 12 years conquering other European leagues such as La Liga (Spain) and Seria A (Italy).

He is believed to bring inspirations to the young stars at the club to become a great player like him. However, there is someone that did not take the opportunity seriously...

It is Mason Greenwood, the supporters believe. The young star has recently been accused of several assaults and crimes against his girlfriend. Manchester United, Nike and FIFA so far have suspended contract with him.
